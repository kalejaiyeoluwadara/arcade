# v0.6 — Game 3: Fill Shot

**Goal:** The area-fill shooter from the original Brick Game "Fill Shot" modes —
the most novel mechanic of the three. Built last because it leans hardest on the
grid model.

## Route & files

- Route: `app/fill-shot/`
- Logic: `games/fillShot/`

## Gameplay

- Static obstacles occupy parts of the playfield at the start.
- Player shoots projectiles that **fill empty cells** (the shot claims cells until
  it hits an obstacle/edge, marking that area as filled).
- Goal: occupy a **target percentage** of the screen (e.g. 75%).
- Moving enemies roam the field — being hit while filling costs a life (and may
  reset the in-progress fill).
- Speeds up / target rises with level.

## Mechanic notes

- Track three cell states: empty · filled · obstacle (plus transient "filling").
- Compute filled-% each tick to detect win; compare against the level target.
- Collision: enemy vs player and enemy vs in-progress fill path.
- Keep it grid-pure — the "fill" is just claiming cells, not a flood animation with
  easing.

## Integrates

- Engine + store + shell. Mode label = "FILL". Score = % filled / cleared bonus.
- Sound call sites (shoot/fill/hit/level-clear/game-over) as stubs for v0.7.

## Out of scope

- Smooth fill animations, particles, power-ups.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Obstacles render; player can claim empty regions by shooting.

- [ ] Enemies move and can end a life / interrupt a fill.
- [ ] Difficulty ramps (speed + target %).
