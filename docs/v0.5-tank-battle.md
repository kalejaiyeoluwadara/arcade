# v0.5 — Game 2: Tank Battle

**Goal:** Top-down tank shooter on a single-screen arena, adding walls and
multi-directional play on top of the proven engine.

## Route & files

- Route: `app/tank/`
- Logic: `games/tank/`

## Gameplay

- **Top-down** view, single-screen arena (no scrolling/camera).
- Player tank = **2×2 cells**; faces a direction; D-pad both moves and aims.
- Fire with A/Space — bullet travels **one cell per tick** in the facing direction.
- **Walls:** destructible (break when shot) and indestructible (block bullets and
  movement). Lay out a simple authentic maze.
- Enemy tanks spawn **from the top**, move and shoot with simple patterns.
- Objective: survive and eliminate enemy tanks.
- Difficulty: faster enemies and higher spawn rate per level.

## New engine usage

- Direction/facing state for player and enemies.
- Wall grid layer + rect collision for 2×2 bodies (collision helpers from v0.2).
- Bullet-vs-wall (destructible removes the cell; indestructible stops the bullet).

## Integrates

- Engine + store + shell as established. Mode label = "TANK".
- Sound call sites (shoot/hit/wall-break/game-over) as stubs for v0.7.

## Out of scope

- Diagonal movement, smooth turning, particle explosions, power-ups.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Tank moves and aims in 4 directions, cell-snapped.
- [ ] Destructible vs indestructible walls behave distinctly.
- [ ] Enemy tanks spawn from top, move, shoot, and can be destroyed.
- [ ] Player loses lives on being hit; game-over + restart works.
- [ ] Difficulty ramps; rendering obeys the 4-color rule.
